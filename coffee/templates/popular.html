A matcha latte is a creamy beverage made by whisking finely ground green tea powder (matcha) with hot water and blending it with steamed or cold milk. Originating as a popular adaptation of traditional Japanese tea rituals, this bright green drink balances a distinct grassy, earthy, and slightly bitter umami flavor profile with smooth, comforting sweetness.

A Ice Latte a refreshing and creamy coffee drink made by combining freshly brewed espresso with cold milk and ice. It provides a smoother, milk-forward alternative to standard iced coffee.

A Cappuchino a traditional Italian espresso-based coffee drink prepared with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and a thick layer of milk foam.

A Frappe The term frape can refer to several completely different concepts depending on whether you are talking about coffee, technology, or internet slang.Most commonly, it is a simplified spelling of frappé, the famous Greek iced coffee drink made from instant coffee, water, sugar, and milk.
